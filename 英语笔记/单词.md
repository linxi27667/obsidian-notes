> _— 通过广泛阅读和探索新领域，它开阔视野并丰富知识。_

# 四、四级翻译保底模板

> [!example]+ 模板一：介绍某事物
> **[事物] has a long history and plays an important role in Chinese culture. In recent years, it has become more and more popular among people. It not only enriches people's daily life, but also helps spread Chinese culture.**
>
> **适用：** 茶文化、传统节日、书法、京剧、传统美食等。

> [!example]+ 模板二：介绍城市发展
> **In recent years, more and more Chinese cities have been making efforts to improve public services. These services provide residents with a more convenient and comfortable living environment. They can better meet people's daily needs and improve their quality of life.**
>
> **适用：** 15分钟生活圈、公共交通、社区服务、城市建设等。

> [!example]+ 模板三：介绍科技发展
> **With the development of science and technology, [事物] has become more and more popular in China. It enables people to do many things more conveniently and efficiently. It has greatly changed people's way of life.**
>
> **适用：** 移动支付、网购、高铁、互联网、人工智能等。

> [!example]+ 模板四：介绍教育学习
> **Education plays an important role in personal development. In recent years, more and more students have begun to pay attention to [主题]. It can not only improve their learning ability, but also help them better adapt to future challenges.**
>
> **适用：** 阅读、在线学习、跨文化交流、终身学习等。
